About Bijapur

Bijapur, officially known as Vijayapura, is a historic city in Karnataka renowned for its magnificent monuments, rich cultural heritage, and Indo-Islamic architecture. The city flourished under the Adil Shahi dynasty and is home to world-famous structures like Gol Gumbaz, Ibrahim Rauza, and Bijapur Fort.

Bijapur stands as a symbol of architectural brilliance, attracting tourists, historians, and architecture lovers from across the globe.

Places to Visit in Bijapur

Discover the timeless monuments and architectural wonders of Vijayapura.

Gol Gumbaz

Famous for its massive dome and whispering gallery, Gol Gumbaz is an architectural masterpiece of the Adil Shahi era.

Ibrahim Rauza

Known as the inspiration for the Taj Mahal, Ibrahim Rauza is admired for its elegant symmetry and carvings.

Bijapur Fort

A massive fort complex that showcases mosques, palaces, and historic gateways from the Deccan Sultanate.

Jama Masjid

One of the largest mosques in India, known for its vast prayer hall and impressive arches.
